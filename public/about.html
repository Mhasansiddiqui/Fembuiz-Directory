Imagine this. You’re a woman, and you’ve suffered from domestic violence, or if you haven’t then you know someone who has, or maybe you’ve just read about it in the media.

Recently, media personality, Mia Freedman posted a Facebook status update highlighting just how unsafe women can feel at times. She arrived home and there was a man on her roof. She wasn’t sure who he was. She didn’t know if he was a burglar, or what was going on. She called her husband and he told her that he was the tradie. Following this, Freedman reflected that women shouldn’t need to feel like they’re in danger.

Well, to go some way to addressing this issue, Fembuiz Directory was set up to solve that very problem. It’s not a charity. It’s a directory that enables women, or anyone for that matter, to search for female tradies to sort out their home repairs or anything else that they need doing. There’s no need to feel unsafe, and there’s no risk that you’re calling on a tradie who may become violent.

We exist for two reasons:

1 – To help women feel safer in their communities.

2 – To enable female business owners and tradies to list their services.

As a business, our mission statement is to empower women to enter the trades or other and go into business. We want to help close the pay gap between men and women. The best way to do that is for women who are currently in the trades, to connect with other women, and for women to know that there are actually women in the trades.

All it takes is one person to spread the word and who knows what will happen? You may be calling a tradie today, but your daughter may become one the next day, or when she grows up. So, take a look around, and you’ll find the tradies that you never knew existed. For years it’s seemed like women don’t enter the trades, and if you’re a female tradie, it can be tough to gain business due to the lack of knowledge of your existence.

Fembuiz is an online directory that features female tradies, business owners and entrepreneurs.

It enables them to list their services and be found by people who may otherwise not know of their existence. It allows community members to find female businesses that aren’t currently getting any publicity.

It’s a platform allowing women to list their products or services, connect with other women, reach clients and increase their public profile. Advertising can be expensive. Fembuiz is a cost effective and affordable way to reach the market and gain contacts in a positive and supportive environment.

We want to help you reach your business goals, so if you’re a woman offering a trade or service simply enquire with us and you’ll be able to list your business with all your contact details. If of course you’re a female looking to engage with a female tradie or female led business, have a look through our directory and you’ll find what you’re looking for.

We cover all industries from trades and services through to marketing, and finance, and even home businesses.

There’s something for everyone.

We want to encourage women to be brave, fore-fill their mission and have the confidence to tell their stories
